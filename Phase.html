COMPANY INTEREST SCHOLARSHIPS
‣ First, the company will enter the school system to pick students depends on their grade and major, then they have to met the student in person to introduce their company. A single students could receive more than one company's "invitation".
‣ Second, the student get to have a choice either to accept the grant and work for the company after college, or to reject it. And one student only able to accept one company's grant.
‣ Third, if the student accept the grant, then student have to sign the document to agree that student will work in the company after graduation.
‣ Fourth, after graduation, student will start working for the company for at least certain period of time. The amount of time should be decided by student and company when they met in phrase one. During this time, company have the ability to discharge the worker if their working didn't meet the expectation. And the worker can leave during in the time in case a student get too much grant and only work for the company 1 day.
