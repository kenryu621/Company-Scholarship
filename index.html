MY INNOVATION
My idea is create a new type of scholarship that brings companies and students together. A scholarship that can benefit the company and the student at the same time.
The scholarship is a grant pay by company, and the company choose which student they gonna give it to. If the student accept the grant, the student will have to work for the company for a certain period of time after their graduation. Any company can enter this program, they can find the student by looking at what major does the student take, what grade do they have, what experience they have, and other information about them in school.
